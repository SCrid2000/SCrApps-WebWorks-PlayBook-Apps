I sincerely hope you enjoy this app. In case you did not read the app description, the purpose of this app is to launch the PlayBook Video Camera. For some reason, the PlayBook does not have a stock icon for the Video Camera; in order to access the video Camera, you must launch the regular camera and then switch to video mode. This app makes accessing the Video Camera simpler, by putting a Video Camera icon right on your homecreen. The app has been optimized to take as little space and use as litte resources as possible.
